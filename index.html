AnalogyForge

AnalogyForge helps researchers test whether a cross-domain analogy really holds up. It uses LLMs to map the source and target mechanisms, highlights strong and weak correspondences, shows where the analogy breaks, and turns the most defensible transfer into a testable hypothesis.
